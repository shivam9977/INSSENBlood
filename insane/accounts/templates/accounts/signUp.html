About Us

Inssen is a open source blood Donation app available for Android smartphones. Inssen searches, notifies and connect thousands of blood donors in three simple steps. Inssen donation app ensures hassle free blood donation and privacy of a blood donor. A blood donor can select any specific date and location to donate blood and find a suitable person who needs blood.
